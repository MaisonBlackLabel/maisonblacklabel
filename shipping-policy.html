Insured Shipping at Our Cost

Maison Black Label arranges and covers the cost of fully insured shipping on every completed sale. All shipments are sent via trusted carriers with tracking and signature confirmation required upon delivery, so your piece is protected from the moment it leaves us until it reaches your hands.

Processing & Handoff

Once a transaction is finalized and payment is confirmed, we process and ship your item promptly. You will receive tracking information as soon as your piece is on its way.

Domestic Shipping

Within the continental United States, fully insured, signature-required delivery is provided at no additional shipping cost to the buyer.

International Shipping

International shipping is available by arrangement. International buyers are responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or fees assessed by their country's customs authority upon arrival. We provide all necessary documentation to support customs clearance.

Risk of Loss

Because every shipment is fully insured, risk of loss or damage in transit is covered until the item is delivered and signed for.

Accepted Payment Methods

We accept Wire Transfer, Zelle, and cash as our standard payment methods. Cryptocurrency and other payment arrangements may be accepted on a case-by-case basis at our discretion. Credit card payments are reserved for established, repeat clients.
