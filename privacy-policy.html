Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Who We Are

Maison Black Label is a luxury watch and exotic asset specialist based in West Palm Beach, Florida. This policy explains how we handle personal information collected through maisonblacklabel.com (the "Site").

Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our contact form, we collect the information you provide directly — typically your name, email address, phone number, and details about the service you are interested in (sourcing, buying, selling, trading, or consigning a timepiece). We do not require you to create an account to browse the Site or submit an inquiry.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry, prepare quotes, facilitate purchases, sales, trades, and consignments, arrange fully insured shipping, and communicate with you about your transaction. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Cookies & Analytics

This Site uses Google Analytics to help us understand site traffic and improve the browsing experience. Google Analytics may use cookies to collect anonymized usage data, such as pages viewed and approximate location. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by adjusting your browser's cookie settings.

Third-Party Services

Our Site is hosted on Netlify, and contact form submissions are processed using Netlify Forms. Fonts are served via Google Fonts. These providers may process limited technical data, such as IP address, as part of standard web hosting operations, governed by their own respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain inquiry and transaction information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, and to comply with applicable recordkeeping or tax obligations.

Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. To make such a request, contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the most recent revision.
